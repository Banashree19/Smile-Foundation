Our Vision

Our Mission

Girls' Education Empowering Girls

Educating the girl child refers to every aspect of education that aims at developing the skill and knowledge of girls and women. This includes the general education at colleges, schools, professional education, technical and vocational educations, health education, etc. Girl child education isn’t one to take for granted and this has made many NGO’s in India to pick up interesting to educate the girl child. The society has witness changes in the status of women. There is now greater emphasis on the education of the girl child and the way that they are educated.
